12 FAQ

Good questions.

How do your engagements actually work?

Most engagements open with a paid 2–3 week Strategy Sprint — audience, positioning, channel plan and a measurement framework — so we build on evidence, not guesswork. From there, clients move onto a rolling monthly growth retainer with strategy, creative, media and reporting under one team, while brand identities and websites run as fixed-scope projects. Retainers are rolling with 30 days' notice — no year-long lock-ins.

What does it cost to work with Hook?

We scope to outcomes, not hours. Strategy sprints and brand/web builds are fixed-fee; growth retainers are tiered by channel mix and the ad spend we manage. You'll get a clear investment range on the first call and a fixed proposal before any commitment — no surprise invoices, ever.

Who's the right fit — what size companies do you work with?

From funded startups to established GCC brands and government-backed entities. The deciding factor isn't size — it's ambition and a real budget to grow. If we're not the right partner for you, we'll say so on the first call and point you somewhere better.

What are GEO / AEO / AIO — and why should I care now?

Discovery is splitting in two. Classic SEO still wins Google's links, but a fast-growing share of search now happens inside AI answers — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot and Google's AI Overviews. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and AIO (AI Optimization) make your brand the source those engines cite — not the one they skip. We engineer your content, entities and schema to win both, because the brands that own the answer in 2026 own the category.

How do you measure success and report?

Every engagement is tied to a north-star business metric — pipeline, revenue, CAC or ROAS — agreed up front. You get a live dashboard wiring together GA4, the ad platforms and your CRM, plus a monthly readout showing what moved, what didn't, and exactly what we're changing next. We optimize for numbers you can take to a board, not vanity impressions.

Do you work in Arabic and across the GCC?

Yes. We're Bahrain-based and build bilingual (Arabic & English) brands and campaigns across Bahrain, Saudi Arabia and the wider GCC — localized for the region's platforms, culture and buying behavior, never translated as an afterthought.

How fast can we start?

Discovery usually begins within a week of our first call, and your Strategy Sprint delivers its first output inside 2–3 weeks. Got a hard deadline — a launch, a campaign, an event? Tell us, and we'll build the plan around it.
